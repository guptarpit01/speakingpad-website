Method

The SpeakingPad method is intentionally practice-first.

Each learner gets a clear path from diagnosis to practice, feedback and repeated improvement.

1

Diagnose

Identify where the speaker gets stuck: confidence, structure, vocabulary, grammar, clarity or delivery.

2

Practice

Use speeches, introductions, presentations and real workplace scenarios to build speaking muscle.

3

Feedback

Receive specific recommendations while mentors actively listen, observe and make notes.

4

Improve

Repeat with sharper structure, stronger delivery and more confidence in real speaking situations.
